Publish your recipes.

After getting creative in the kitchen, don't just write up what you made on paper. Publishing on Gingrsnap allows you to easily share what you've created. The next time a friend asks you for a recipe, you can send them a link instead of transcribing it into an email.

Track what you've been making.

Keep track of what you cook by recording "makes" on Gingrsnap. Recipes accumulate makes, giving an organic measurement of a dish's popularity.

Collaborate on recipes by leaving tips.

Find a new twist to someone's dish on Gingrsnap? Leave a tip on the recipe so others can try out your alteration.
